Ayugi Sophia

Ayugi Sophia Angela was born on 23rd August to the late
Everline Akinyi Ayugi and Thomas Opiyo Ayugi.
She is 23years old, a slim, tall, black lady.
She is a Kenyan by birth, she stays in Homabay county.
She is the fourth born among six children.
She is always a quiet girl
but bad when annoyed.Sophia is a humble lady,
like playing volleyball, traveling to new places and making new
friends but an introvert and trying to be an extrovert.
She believes in herself that,when she slides, she hasn't fallen so
she can stand and move on She finished her secondary education in 2019,
in which she did all her best, but she didn't manage to join a collage
by Gods grace, she was chosen to be in AkiraChix as one
of the students learning programming languages. And she is proud
to be one of the be in that compound and to do all her best.
